Sleek Riddles

I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have no body, but I come alive with the wind. What am I?

An echo

I have cities, but no houses. I have forests, but no trees. I have rivers, but no water. What am I?

A map

I am taken from a mine and shut in a wooden case, from which I am never released. I am used by scholars and farmers alike. What am I?

A pencil

What has a heart that doesn't beat?

A deck of cards

The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?

Footsteps

What has keys but can't open locks?

A piano

I have keys but no locks. I have space but no room. You can enter but can't go outside. What am I?

A keyboard
